By George Herbert

Ah, my dear angry Lord,  
Since thou dost love, yet strike;  
Cast down, yet help afford;  
Sure I will do the like.  
  
I will complain, yet praise;  
I will bewail, approve;  
And all my sour-sweet days  
I will lament and love.
